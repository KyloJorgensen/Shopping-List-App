To add a new Item to the shopping list click the text "Click here to add new item." at the bottom of the list.

To edit an item just click that the text of that item.

To delete that item just click the trash that is left of the item when you click on it.

To cross an item off your list just click the box next to that item.

To uncross an item off your list just click the box with a check in it left of that item.
