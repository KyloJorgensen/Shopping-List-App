Shopping List

To add a new Item to the shopping list:

Click the text "Click here to add new item." at the bottom of the list. Then click the plus sign to left.

To edit an item:

Click the text of that item and type. When complete click the plus sign to the left.

To delete an item:

First click the text of that item. Then click the trash that is left of the item.

To cross an item off your list:

Click the box next to that item.

To uncross an item off your list:

Click the box with a check in it left of that item.
